Frequently Asked Questions

What are the operating hours of the coffee shop?

Our operating hours are Monday to Friday from 7 AM to 6 PM and weekends from 9 AM to 4 PM.

Do you offer free Wi-Fi?

Yes, we provide free Wi-Fi for our customers.

Can you provide a menu and price list?

Certainly, you can check our complete menu and price list on our website.

Does the coffee shop have indoor and outdoor seating?

Yes, we offer comfortable indoor seating and a spacious outdoor terrace for you to enjoy your coffee in different environments.

Do you provide takeout and delivery services?

Yes, you can place takeout and delivery orders through our website or partnered delivery platforms.

Does the coffee shop accept customers using their reusable cups?

Absolutely, we encourage sustainable practices, and you can enjoy a discount when using your own reusable cup.

What is the coffee shop's policy for returns and refunds if a customer is dissatisfied with a product?

If you are not satisfied with our products, please let us know immediately, and we will be happy to offer an exchange or a full refund.

Can I reserve a table in advance?

Unfortunately, we do not accept table reservations at the moment. Seating is on a first-come, first-served basis.

How can customers contact the coffee shop?

You can reach us through email (info@coffee-shop.com), phone (+123 456 789), or in-person at the shop. We look forward to hearing from you.

What health and safety measures does the coffee shop have in place?

We strictly adhere to health and safety standards, including regular cleaning, employee health checks, and social distancing measures, to ensure your safety.
